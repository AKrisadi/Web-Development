Biography

Linus Benedict Trovalds is a Finnish software engineer who is the creator and, historically, the lead developer of the Linux kernel, used by Linux distributions and other operating systems such as Android. He also created the distributed version control system Git.

Did you know ?

Torvalds is married to Tove Torvalds, a six-time Finnish national karate champion, whom he met in late 1993.
